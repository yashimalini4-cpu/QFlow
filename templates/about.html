About QFlow

QFlow is a Cloud-Based Smart Queue Management System designed to reduce waiting time and improve customer experience by allowing users to join queues online and monitor their queue status in real time.


Objectives

  • Reduce physical waiting lines.
  • Provide digital token generation.
  • Enable real-time queue monitoring.
  • Improve efficiency for organizations and customers.


Key Features

  • Customer Registration & Login
  • Secure Email OTP Verification
  • Online Token Booking
  • Live Queue Progress
  • Organization Dashboard
  • Real-Time Queue Management
